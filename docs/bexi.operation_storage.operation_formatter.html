bexi.operation_storage.operation_formatter module¶

bexi.operation_storage.operation_formatter.decode_operation(operation)[source]¶

The given operation comes directly from the blockchain and is here reformatted. This is done to better suit the needs of our processing and also due to the fact that the resulting operations dict can’t have nested dicts.

Parameters:operation (dict) – operation as given by blockchain monitor
bexi.operation_storage.operation_formatter.validate_operation(operation)[source]¶

Validates the given reformatted operation against the json schema given by operation_schema.json

Parameters:operation – operation formatted as returned by decode_operation()
